Welcome to the Rules page!

  1. The player must select one of the three options: rock, paper or scissors
  2. If the player selects rock and the computer selects paper, the computer will win
  3. If the player selects rock and the computer selects scissors, the computer will lose
  4. If the player selects paper and the computer selects scissors, the computer will win
  5. If the player selects paper and the computer selects rock, the computer will lost
  6. If the player selects scissors and the computer selects rock, the computer will win
  7. If the player selects scissors and the computer selects paper, the computer will lose
